The RUHH Ritual

How to wear your soul.

I
Pulse Points
Apply to wrists, neck, and behind the ears — where warmth amplifies the scent.
II
Don't Rub
Let the fragrance settle naturally. Rubbing breaks the top notes and shortens the life.
III
Layer It
Apply after a shower on moisturised skin. Hydration locks the scent for up to 12 hours.
IV
Store with care
Keep away from heat and light. A cool, dark place preserves every note perfectly.
